<p><p>Carbon fiber is extensively used in the automotive industry due to its lightweight and high strength properties. Various types of processes are used for manufacturing carbon fiber components. Resin Transfer Molding involves injecting resin into a closed mold containing carbon fiber. Vacuum Infusion Processing utilizes vacuum pressure to infuse resin into carbon fiber layers. Injection Molding uses molten resin injected into a mold cavity containing carbon fiber. Compression Molding involves placing carbon fiber sheets into a mold and compressing them under heat and pressure. Other processes include pre-preg molding and filament winding.</p></p>
