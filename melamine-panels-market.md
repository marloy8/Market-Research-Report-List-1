<p><p>Melamine panels are widely used in the construction and furniture industries for their durability and aesthetic appeal. Particle panels are a type of melamine panel made from recycled wood particles mixed with resin and pressed into sheets. These panels are cost-effective and commonly used for shelves, cabinets, and flooring. Density panels, on the other hand, are made from high-density fiberboard (HDF) and are known for their strength and stability. They are often used in applications that require a smooth and hard surface, such as tabletops and wall panels.</p></p>
