<p><p>Biomass transformer oil market primarily consists of two types: natural transformer fluid and synthetic transformer oil. On the other hand, synthetic transformer oil is chemically synthesized and offers higher performance in terms of stability and durability. It is widely used in high-voltage applications and provides better heat transfer and electrical insulation.
