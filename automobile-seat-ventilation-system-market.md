<p><p>The automobile seat ventilation system market is divided into two types: blow and suction. In the blow market, air is blown through the seat to provide ventilation and coolness. This system uses fans or blowers to circulate air. On the other hand, the suction market operates by sucking air from the seat, creating a vacuum effect. This system uses air pumps or suction devices to remove hot air and maintain a comfortable temperature. Both systems aim to improve passenger comfort and reduce sweat and discomfort while driving.</p></p>
