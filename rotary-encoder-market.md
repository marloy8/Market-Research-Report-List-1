<p><ul><li>Incremental Rotary Encoders</li><li>Absolute Rotary Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary encoder market can be classified into two main types: incremental rotary encoders and absolute rotary encoders. Incremental rotary encoders provide relative position information by measuring the changes in rotation, typically in the form of pulse signals. They are widely used in industrial applications such as robotics and motion control systems. On the other hand, absolute rotary encoders provide absolute position information, enabling precise positioning without the need for a reference point. They are commonly found in applications that require accurate positioning, such as CNC machines and medical equipment.</p></p>
