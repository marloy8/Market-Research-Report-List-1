<p><p>The postoperative local pain relief system market consists of two main types: disposable pain relief pumps and electronic pain relief pumps. Disposable pain relief pumps are single-use devices that deliver pain medication directly to the surgical site, providing localized relief. On the other hand, electronic pain relief pumps are reusable devices that can be programmed to deliver medication at specific intervals, usually through an implanted catheter. Both types of pain relief pumps aim to alleviate postoperative pain and improve patient comfort during the recovery process.</p></p>
