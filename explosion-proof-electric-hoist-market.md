<p><p>The explosion-proof electric hoist market includes two main types: chain hoists and wire rope hoists. Chain hoists are commonly used for lifting heavy loads in industries, construction sites, and warehouses. They consist of a chain connected to a lifting hook, which is powered by an electric motor. On the other hand, wire rope hoists utilize a metal wire rope and a drum to lift objects. These hoists are widely used in industries that require higher lifting capacities and longer lifting heights. Both types are designed to be explosion-proof, ensuring safety in hazardous environments.</p></p>
