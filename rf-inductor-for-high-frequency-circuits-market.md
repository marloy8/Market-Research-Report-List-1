<p><p>The RF inductor market includes three main types: Film Type, Wire Wound Type, and Multilayer Type. Film Type inductors are made by depositing a thin film of conductive material onto a non-conductive substrate, offering high precision and stability. Wire Wound Type inductors are built by winding conductive wire around a core, providing high inductance values. Multilayer Type inductors feature multiple layers of conductive material, offering compact size and high reliability. Each type caters to specific requirements of high-frequency circuits, ensuring efficient performance and meeting diverse application demands.</p></p>
