<p><p>PET film, also known as Polyethylene Terephthalate film, is used in various industries for packaging, electrical insulation, and other applications. There are two main types of PET films: BoPET and CPET. BoPET (Biaxially-Oriented Polyethylene Terephthalate) is produced through a biaxial stretching process, resulting in a strong and transparent film used in packaging, labeling, and graphic applications. CPET (Crystallized Polyethylene Terephthalate) is a rigid and heat-resistant film used for food trays, oven-safe containers, and microwaveable packaging. Both types cater to different market needs based on their specific properties and applications.</p></p>
