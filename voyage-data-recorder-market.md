<p><p>The Voyage Data Recorder (VDR) market consists of two main types: General Voyage Data Recorder (GVDR) and Simplified Voyage Data Recorder (SVDR). The GVDR is a comprehensive system that records and stores various data, including audio, video, and ship's parameters for analysis after an incident or accident at sea. Both types serve as vital tools for investigating and improving safety measures in the maritime industry.</p></p>
