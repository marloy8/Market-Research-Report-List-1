<p><p>Silicon steel sheets are used in various industries due to their magnetic properties. There are two main types in the market: oriented electrical steel and non-grain oriented electrical steel. Oriented electrical steel is specifically designed to have a preferred direction of magnetic properties, making it ideal for transformers and motors. In contrast, non-grain oriented electrical steel does not have a preferred direction and is commonly used in generators and magnetic cores. Both types play significant roles in different applications within the electrical industry.</p></p>
