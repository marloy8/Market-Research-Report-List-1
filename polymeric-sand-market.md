It offers several advantages over traditional sand, such as better resistance to erosion, weed growth, and insect infestation.</p><p>The current outlook of the polymeric sand market is positive and is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. This type of sand helps stabilize pavers, preventing weed growth and erosion. On the other hand, Polymeric Dust refers to a finer version of Polymeric Sand, used mainly for filling narrow joints. While both products serve the same purpose, the main difference lies in their particle size, with Polymeric Dust being finer and more suitable for smaller joint gaps.</p></p>
