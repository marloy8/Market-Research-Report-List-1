<p><p>The gas separation membrane filtration market is categorized into three types: hollow fiber, spiral wound, and others. Hollow fiber membranes are made up of small tubes with a porous structure, allowing gas to pass through while retaining impurities. Spiral wound membranes consist of multiple layers wound together, providing a large surface area for gas separation. Other types include flat sheet membranes and tubular membranes, each offering specific advantages for different applications. These membrane filtration technologies are used in various industries such as oil and gas, chemical processing, and water treatment, to separate gases and purify them efficiently.</p></p>
