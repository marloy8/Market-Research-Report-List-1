<p><p>One-component RTV rubber is a type of rubber commonly used for sealing and bonding purposes. It is available in two market types: addition type and condensation type. Addition type RTV rubber cures through an addition reaction, meaning it requires a curing agent to harden. On the other hand, condensation type RTV rubber cures through a condensation reaction, releasing by-products during the curing process. Both types offer excellent flexibility, adhesive properties, and resistance to heat and chemicals, making them suitable for various industries.</p></p>
