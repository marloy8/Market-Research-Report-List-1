<p><p>Scale inhibitor for refining is a chemical compound used in the oil and gas industry to prevent the formation of mineral scales in refining processes. Mineral scales, such as calcium carbonate, calcium sulfate, and barium sulfate, can build up on the equipment surfaces in refineries, leading to reduced efficiency and equipment damage. Scale inhibitors work by interfering with the crystal formation and growth of these minerals, preventing them from sticking to surfaces.</p><p>The global scale inhibitor for refining market is projected to witness substantial growth in the coming years.
