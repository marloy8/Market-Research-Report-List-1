<p><p>Closed blood sampling systems are medical devices used to collect blood samples from patients in a closed and controlled environment. These systems enable the collection of arterial blood samples in a closed and controlled manner, minimizing the risk of arterial line-associated bloodstream infections. The demand for closed arterial blood sampling systems is likely to increase as healthcare facilities focus on reducing complications associated with arterial line insertions.</p><p>Overall, the closed blood sampling systems market is expected to witness substantial growth in the coming years, driven by the need for safer and more efficient blood collection procedures.
