<p><p>Heavy Duty Coating market includes three major types: solvent-based, water-based, and powder coating. Solvent-based coatings are made by dissolving synthetic resins in organic solvents, offering excellent adhesion and resistance to chemicals and weather conditions. Water-based coatings use water as a solvent, reducing VOC emissions and providing good durability and flexibility. Powder coatings are applied as a dry powder and then heated to form a durable finish, offering high corrosion resistance, excellent adhesion, and environmental benefits due to their solvent-free nature.</p></p>
