<p><p>The runway sweeper market can be divided into two main types: ride-on runway sweepers and towed runway sweepers. Ride-on runway sweepers are designed for operators to ride on and maneuver around the runway, equipped with brushes and suction systems to remove debris and dirt effectively. On the other hand, towed runway sweepers are pulled behind a vehicle and are typically larger, allowing for a higher capacity to collect debris. Both types serve the purpose of maintaining clean and safe runways but differ in terms of operation and size.</p></p>
