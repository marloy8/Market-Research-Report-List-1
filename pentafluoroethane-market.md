The growing emphasis on energy-efficient solutions and the introduction of stringent regulations regarding greenhouse gas emissions also act as catalysts for the adoption of pentafluoroethane as a refrigerant.</p><p>However, the market also faces several challenges. The high price of pentafluoroethane compared to other refrigerants and the availability of substitutes, such as hydrofluoroolefins (HFOs), pose potential hindrances to market growth.
