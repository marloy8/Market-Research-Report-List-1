<p><p>Jockey Box is a type of portable beer system commonly used in outdoor events. It comes in two market types: coil cooler and cold-plate cooler. The coil cooler utilizes a long stainless steel coil immersed in ice water to cool the beer as it flows through the coil, providing a consistent temperature. On the other hand, the cold-plate cooler consists of a rectangular metal plate filled with a refrigerant, ensuring quick and efficient cooling for multiple beverage lines simultaneously. Both types are popular choices for dispensing cold beverages at parties, festivals, and other outdoor gatherings.</p></p>
