<p><ul><li>Thermoset</li><li>Termoplastic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The organic filler market is divided into two types: thermoset and thermoplastic. Thermoset organic filler refers to fillers that are used in products made from materials that undergo irreversibly hardening when cured, such as epoxy or polyester resins. These fillers enhance mechanical strength, thermal stability, and chemical resistance of the products. On the other hand, thermoplastic organic fillers are used in materials that soften when heated and harden when cooled, such as polyethylene or polypropylene. These fillers improve the physical properties and processability of the materials.</p></p>
