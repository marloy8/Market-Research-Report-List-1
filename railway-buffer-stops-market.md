<p><ul><li>Frictional Buffer Stops</li><li>Fixed Buffer Stops</li><li>Hydraulic/Mechanical Buffer Stops</li><li>Wheel Stops</li><li>Folding Buffer Stops</li><li>Special Designed Buffer Stops</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The railway buffer stops market includes various types of buffer stops used to ensure safety and prevent trains from colliding with the end of a track. Frictional buffer stops provide resistance to the train's movement, while fixed buffer stops are immovable barriers. Hydraulic/mechanical buffer stops use hydraulic or mechanical energy to absorb impact. Wheel stops are designed to halt the trains by engaging with their wheels. Folding buffer stops can be folded down to allow trains to pass. Special designed buffer stops are customized to meet specific requirements.</p></p>
