<p><p>The wood pellet market is divided into two main types: white pellets and black pellets. White pellets are made from compressed sawdust and have a lighter color. They are widely used for residential heating and as a renewable energy source. In contrast, black pellets, also known as torrefied pellets, are made from heat-treated biomass and have a darker appearance. They have higher energy densities and can be used for power generation in coal-fired power plants, offering a greener alternative to traditional coal. Both types contribute to the growing demand for sustainable energy solutions.</p></p>
