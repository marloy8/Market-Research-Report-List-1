<p><p>The pharmaceutical borosilicate glass tubes market consists of two main types: alkaline borosilicate glass tubes and neutral borosilicate glass tubes. Alkaline borosilicate glass tubes have a higher level of alkaline earth metals in their composition, making them highly resistant to thermal shock and chemical corrosion, thus suitable for pharmaceutical packaging. On the other hand, neutral borosilicate glass tubes have lower levels of alkaline earth metals, resulting in lower thermal expansion and higher hydrolytic resistance, making them ideal for injectable drug packaging.</p></p>
