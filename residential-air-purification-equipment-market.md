<p><p>The residential air purification equipment market consists of different types of devices designed to improve indoor air quality. High-efficiency Particulate Air (HEPA) filters trap airborne particles, ensuring cleaner air. Electrostatic Precipitators (ESPs) use an electrostatic charge to attract and remove particles from the air. Ionizers release negatively charged ions to neutralize airborne contaminants. Ozone generators produce ozone molecules to eliminate odors and kill bacteria. These devices offer varying levels of effectiveness and consumers should choose what best suits their air quality needs.</p></p>
