<p><p>The Automotive Engine Chain market consists of two main types: roller chain and silent chain. Roller chain is a type of mechanical chain that uses cylindrical rollers to provide smooth and efficient power transmission. It is commonly used in automotive engines due to its durability and ability to handle high speeds and heavy loads. On the other hand, silent chain, also known as inverted tooth chain, is designed to run smoothly and quietly. It is commonly used in timing and balance shaft applications, providing precise timing and reduced noise levels in automotive engines.</p></p>
