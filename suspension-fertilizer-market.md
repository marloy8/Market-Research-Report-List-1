There are two main types of suspension fertilizers: compound suspension fertilizer and pure inorganic suspension fertilizer. </p><p>Compound suspension fertilizers are made up of a combination of different nutrients such as nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium. They provide a balanced supply of essential nutrients for plant growth and are commonly used in agriculture.</p><p>On the other hand, pure inorganic suspension fertilizers are composed solely of inorganic nutrients and do not contain any organic matter. These fertilizers are typically used in specific agricultural practices where precise and targeted nutrient application is required.</p></p>
