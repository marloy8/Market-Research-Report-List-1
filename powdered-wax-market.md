Polyethylene wax is commonly utilized as a processing aid and lubricant in plastic and rubber industries. Lastly, polypropylene wax finds applications in the production of candles, electrical appliances, and as a modifier in various industries. These different types cater to diverse market needs and offer specific characteristics and benefits.</p></p>
