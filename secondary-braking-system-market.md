<p><strong>The Secondary Braking System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Retarder</li><li>Electromagnetic Retarder</li><li>Engine Brake</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The secondary braking system market consists of various types of technologies. Hydraulic retarders use oil pressure to slow down the vehicle, providing effective braking force. Electromagnetic retarders generate resistance via electromagnetic induction, reducing the vehicle's speed. Engine brakes use the engine's compression to create resistance and slow down the vehicle. Other types of secondary braking systems include eddy current retarders, which use magnetic fields to generate resistance, and hydraulic power recovery systems, which convert kinetic energy into hydraulic energy for braking. These technologies offer diverse options for enhancing vehicle safety and control.</p></p>
