<p><p>The automotive active seatbelt market offers various types of seatbelts for enhanced safety. Belt-In-Seat refers to seatbelts integrated into the seats, providing convenient and automatic restraint. Six-Point Belt includes an additional two belts securing the driver's or passenger's lower body. Five-Point Belt consists of shoulder and lap belts, along with an additional strap between the legs for added security. Four-Point Belt features shoulder and lap belts without the additional leg strap. Three-Point Belt is a standard seatbelt, while Two-Point Belt only provides lap restraint without a shoulder strap.</p></p>
