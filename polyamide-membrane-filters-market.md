<p><p>The polyamide membrane filters market can be categorized into three types based on the pore sizes of the filters. Filters with pore sizes below 0.45 µm are used to remove very small particles and microorganisms from liquids. Filters with pore sizes ranging from 0.45 to 6 µm are suitable for fine filtration applications. Lastly, filters with pore sizes above 6 µm are designed for coarse filtration and are used to remove larger particles and sediments. These different types of filters cater to varying filtration requirements in industries such as pharmaceuticals, biotechnology, and food and beverage.</p></p>
