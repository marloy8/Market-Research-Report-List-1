<p><p>Double regulating valves, also known as balancing valves, are used in HVAC systems to regulate the flow and pressure of fluids such as water or steam. These valves consist of a valve body with two adjustable pressure regulators that allow for precise control of the flow rate. They are primarily used in heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems to ensure proper distribution of fluids and maintain desired conditions in different zones or components of the system.</p><p>The global double regulating valves market is expected to witness significant growth in the coming years. Factors driving this growth include the increasing adoption of HVAC systems in residential, commercial, and industrial buildings, as well as the growing focus on energy efficiency and sustainability. </p><p>Rising concerns regarding energy consumption and the need to optimize system performance have led to a higher demand for double regulating valves.
