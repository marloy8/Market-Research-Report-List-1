Hydraulic VRCS uses hydraulic power to control valves, Electro-Hydraulic VRCS combines hydraulic and electrical systems, Pneumatic VRCS utilizes compressed air to control valves, and Electric VRCS relies on electric power for valve control. Each market type differs in terms of the power source used, offering various benefits and applicability based on specific industry requirements.</p></p>
