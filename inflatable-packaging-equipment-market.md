<p><ul><li>Bubble Sheet Output</li><li>Air Pillow Output</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The inflatable packaging equipment market includes two main types: bubble sheet output and air pillow output. Bubble sheet output refers to the production of packaging materials that consist of small bubbles of air trapped between layers of plastic film. This type of packaging is commonly used for protecting fragile items during transportation. On the other hand, air pillow output involves the manufacturing of flexible plastic bags that can be filled with air to create protective cushions. These air pillows are frequently employed for void-fill applications inside shipping boxes.</p></p>
