<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The In-Mold Labeled (IML) Containers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blow IML Containers</li><li>Injection IML Containers</li><li>Thermoforming IML Containers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The In-Mold Labeled (IML) Containers Market includes three main types: Blow IML Containers, Injection IML Containers, and Thermoforming IML Containers. Blow IML Containers are produced by molding molten plastic into a hollow container shape using compressed air. Injection IML Containers involve injecting molten plastic into a mold and then applying the label. Thermoforming IML Containers are created by heating a plastic sheet, placing it in a mold, and then applying the label. These different methods allow for the efficient production of durable and aesthetically pleasing containers with embedded labels.</p></p>
