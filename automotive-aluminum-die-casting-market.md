<p><ul><li>Hot Chamber Type</li><li>Cold Chamber Type</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Automotive aluminum die casting market types include hot chamber type and cold chamber type. In the hot chamber type, the casting machine is immersed in a pool of molten metal, with a piston injecting the liquid metal into the die. This type is suitable for alloys with low melting points, such as zinc. In the cold chamber type, the molten metal is ladled into a separate chamber and then injected into the die under high pressure. This type is commonly used for aluminum alloys with high melting points, ensuring better casting quality.</p></p>
