<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Refractory Alumina Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Calcined Alumina</li><li>Fused Alumina</li><li>Tabular Alumina</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The refractory alumina market is categorized into several types, including calcined alumina, fused alumina, tabular alumina, and others. Calcined alumina is produced by heating alumina hydrate, resulting in improved hardness and thermal properties. Fused alumina is manufactured by fusing alumina at high temperatures, offering high purity and hardness. Tabular alumina, on the other hand, is produced by sintering ground alumina into sheets, providing excellent thermal shock resistance. Lastly, the "others" category encompasses various types of alumina products used in refractory applications, such as spinel and white fused alumina.</p></p>
