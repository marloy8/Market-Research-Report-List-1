<p><p>Automated blood component separators are medical devices used to separate whole blood into its components such as red blood cells, platelets, and plasma. Single press separators are machines that separate blood into two components, typically red blood cells and plasma, while double press separators separate blood into three components, including red blood cells, platelets, and plasma. These separators use centrifugal forces to separate the blood components based on their density. Single press separators are generally simpler in design and cheaper, while double press separators offer more versatility and functionality.</p></p>
