<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Automotive Electric Power Assist Equipment (EPAS) market includes two popular types: Column EPS and Pinion EPS. Column EPS refers to electric power assist systems where the electric motor is mounted on the steering column. This design offers advantages like simplified installation, enhanced safety, and reduced vibrations. In contrast, Pinion EPS refers to systems where the electric motor is placed directly on the steering gear pinion. This design provides better steering feel and feedback, improved responsiveness, and higher energy efficiency. Both Column EPS and Pinion EPS enhance vehicle steering performance and reduce driver effort.</p></p>
