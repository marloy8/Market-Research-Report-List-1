<p><p>The Vehicle Radiation Monitoring System market consists of two types: fixed and mobile. Fixed systems are installed at specific locations and continuously monitor radiation levels in vehicles passing through checkpoints. On the other hand, mobile systems are portable and can be deployed on various vehicles to monitor radiation on-the-go. These systems are often used by law enforcement agencies, emergency response teams, and customs officers to detect radioactive materials during vehicle inspections.</p></p>
