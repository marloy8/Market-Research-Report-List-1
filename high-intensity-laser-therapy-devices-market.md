<p><p>High-intensity laser therapy devices market types include solid-state lasers, gas lasers, and liquid lasers. Solid-state lasers use a solid medium, usually a crystal or glass, to generate a laser beam. Gas lasers use a gas mixture as the medium, such as helium-neon or carbon dioxide, to produce the laser beam. Liquid lasers use a liquid medium, typically a dye solution, to generate the laser beam. Each of these types has its advantages and applications in various industries, such as medical, manufacturing, and research.</p></p>
