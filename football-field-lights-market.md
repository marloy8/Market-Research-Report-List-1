<p><p>The football field lights market can be categorized into two main types: halogen lighting and LED lighting. Halogen lighting involves the use of halogen gas to produce light, which is then directed onto the football field. On the other hand, LED lighting utilizes light-emitting diodes to generate bright and efficient light. Both types of lighting offer their own advantages and disadvantages. While halogen lighting is more affordable initially, LED lighting is more energy-efficient, has a longer lifespan, and provides better visibility on the football field.</p></p>
