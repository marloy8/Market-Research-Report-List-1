<p><strong>The Resin Dental Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Macrofilled Filler</li><li>Microfilled Filler</li><li>Hybrid Filler</li><li>Nanofilled Filler</li><li>Bulk Filler</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The resin dental material market includes various types of fillers used in dental procedures. Macrofilled fillers consist of large particles that provide strength but lack aesthetics. Microfilled fillers are made up of small particles and are used for front teeth restorations due to their superior aesthetics. Hybrid fillers combine both macrofilled and microfilled fillers to achieve a balance between strength and aesthetics. Nanofilled fillers contain the smallest particles, enhancing both aesthetics and strength. Bulk filler is a type of resin that can be applied as a single layer, reducing the application time during dental procedures.</p></p>
