<p><p>The monofilament extruding machine market can be categorized into two types: PLC linkage and stand-alone control. PLC linkage refers to machines that are integrated with a programmable logic controller (PLC) system, allowing for automated control and monitoring of the extrusion process. This type of machine is commonly used in large-scale industrial applications. On the other hand, stand-alone control machines operate independently without the need for a PLC system. These machines are typically used in smaller-scale operations or where manual control is preferred.</p></p>
