<p><p>Architectural double glazed solar control glass is available in two types: absorbing solar control glass and reflective solar control glass. Absorbing solar control glass is designed to absorb and reduce solar heat radiation, allowing natural light to pass through while minimizing heat transfer. On the other hand, reflective solar control glass has a metallic coating that reflects a significant amount of solar radiation, reducing heat absorption and glare. Both types of glass help to achieve energy efficiency by regulating heat and light transmission in buildings.</p></p>
