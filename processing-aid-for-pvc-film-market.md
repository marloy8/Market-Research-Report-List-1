<p><p>Processing aid for PVC film is a substance used to enhance the production process of PVC film. There are two types of processing aids available: prill and powder. Prill processing aids are in the form of small spherical pellets, while powder processing aids are in the form of fine particles. Both types are added to the PVC film production process to improve the film's properties, such as melt flow, thermal stability, and surface quality. These aids facilitate uniform mixing, prevent fusion defects, and increase production efficiency. Prill and powder processing aids are essential ingredients in the PVC film market.</p></p>
