<p><p>High Temperature Resistant ABS Resin is divided into two market types based on the Vicat Softening Temperature (VST). The first type is VST 100℃~109℃, which indicates that the ABS resin can withstand temperatures between 100℃ and 109℃ before softening. The second type is VST 110℃~119℃, which means the resin can resist temperatures ranging from 110℃ to 119℃ before undergoing softening. These market types help customers choose the right ABS resin suitable for their specific temperature requirements.</p></p>
