<p><p>The flocculant for mining market can be divided into two main types: inorganic flocculant and organic flocculant. Inorganic flocculants are derived from mineral compounds and are commonly used in mining processes to enhance solid-liquid separation. They work by creating large particles that easily settle at the bottom of the solution, simplifying the filtration process. On the other hand, organic flocculants are derived from natural or synthetic polymers and are used to improve the settling characteristics of suspended particles in mining wastewater, aiding in efficient water treatment processes.</p></p>
