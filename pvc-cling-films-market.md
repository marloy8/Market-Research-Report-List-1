<p><p>PVC cling film is available in various thicknesses, which cater to different market segments. The types of PVC cling films are categorized based on their thickness. Films up to 10 microns are thin and economical, generally suitable for household purposes like food covering. Films ranging from 10-15 microns offer slightly better strength and are used for both household and commercial purposes. Films between 15-20 microns are thicker and cater to the commercial sector, providing enhanced protection to food items. Films above 20 microns are heavy-duty, primarily used in industries where durability and strength are paramount.</p></p>
