<p><p>Urea is a widely used nitrogen-rich fertilizer for crops. Within the urea market, there are two primary types: large granule urea and small granule urea. Large granule urea refers to urea granules that are larger in size, typically used for broadcasting or top-dressing applications in agriculture. On the other hand, small granule urea refers to urea granules that are smaller in size and are often used in precision farming techniques, such as fertigation or foliar application. These different market types cater to varying farming practices and preferences of farmers.</p></p>
