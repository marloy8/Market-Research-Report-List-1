<p><p>Chemical air filters are available in two main types: metal frame and plastic frame. Metal frame filters are constructed using a sturdy metal frame, making them highly durable and ideal for heavy-duty applications. They provide excellent chemical filtration and can withstand high temperatures and harsh environments. They offer good chemical filtration but may be less durable than metal frame filters. Both types are effective in removing harmful chemicals from the air.</p></p>
