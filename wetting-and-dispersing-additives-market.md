<p><p>The wetting and dispersing additives market can be categorized into two types based on the media in which they are used - for water-based and solvent-based media. Wetting additives are used to reduce the surface tension of a liquid, allowing it to spread more easily on a solid surface. Dispersing additives are used to evenly distribute solid particles in a liquid, preventing clumping or sedimentation. These additives improve the stability, flow, and performance of paints, coatings, inks, and other liquid formulations, either in water-based or solvent-based systems.</p></p>
