<p><p>Geosynthetics are materials used in civil engineering projects for their enhanced properties and durability. Geotextiles are permeable fabrics that provide filtration and drainage, commonly used in road construction and erosion control. Geomembranes are impermeable sheets used for containment applications such as landfill liners. Geogrids are high-strength materials utilized for soil reinforcement in retaining walls and road stabilization. The "Others" category includes products like geonets, geocomposites, and geocells, which serve various functions such as drainage, filtration, and erosion control in construction projects.</p></p>
