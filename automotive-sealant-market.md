<p><p>The automotive sealant market is classified into four main types: acrylic, PVA, silicones, and polyurethanes. Acrylic sealants are known for their excellent adhesion and flexibility, making them suitable for a wide range of automotive applications. PVA sealants offer good water resistance and are commonly used for sealing joints and seams. Silicones are highly flexible and have excellent heat resistance, making them ideal for engine and exhaust system seals. Polyurethanes provide superior durability and are often used for bonding and sealing automotive parts.</p></p>
