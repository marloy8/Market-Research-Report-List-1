<p><p>Consumer Goods AGV (Automated Guided Vehicle) market can be categorized into three types based on their functionality and application. </p><p>1. Automated Forklift Type AGVs are designed to handle and transport heavy loads, typically in warehouses or manufacturing facilities. They are equipped with lifting forks similar to traditional forklifts, providing efficient material handling and stacking capabilities. </p><p>2. Unit Load Type AGVs are used for transporting entire pallet loads or containers within industrial settings. They are versatile in their ability to pick up, move, and stack loads, making them ideal for applications like order fulfillment and sorting tasks. </p><p>3. Tugger Type AGVs are designed specifically to tow or push heavy carts or trailers, usually in a logistics or assembly line environment. They are equipped with towing mechanisms and perform tasks such as line-side delivery, cart transportation, and material replenishment. Overall, these different AGV types offer efficient and automated solutions for handling consumer goods in various industrial settings.</p></p>
