<p><p>The wood-flooring adhesives market offers three main types of adhesives: water-based, solvent-based, and urethane-based. Water-based adhesives are environmentally friendly and have low VOC emissions, making them suitable for indoor applications. Solvent-based adhesives are known for their strong initial tack and fast drying time, making them ideal for time-sensitive projects. Urethane-based adhesives provide excellent bonding strength and flexibility, making them suitable for high traffic areas. Each type offers unique benefits, allowing consumers to choose the adhesive that best suits their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
