<p><p>The synthetic hydrocarbon grease market can be categorized into three types based on viscosity levels. Low viscosity greases have a thin consistency and are suitable for applications where low resistance to movement is required. Median viscosity greases have a moderate consistency, making them suitable for general-purpose applications. On the other hand, high viscosity greases have a thick consistency and are ideal for heavy-duty applications that require high resistance to movement. These viscosity levels help in determining the appropriate grease for different usage scenarios, ensuring optimal lubrication and performance.</p></p>
