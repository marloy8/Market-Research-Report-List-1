<p><p>Insomnia monitoring equipment refers to devices used to track and measure sleep patterns in individuals suffering from insomnia. Actigraphy sensors are a type of equipment that use wristwatches or wristbands to detect movement and light exposure, providing information about sleep-wake cycles. PSG (polysomnography) devices are advanced systems that record brain waves, heart rate, breathing, and muscle activity during sleep. These devices offer detailed analysis of sleep stages and disorders. Other types of insomnia monitoring equipment include sleep diaries and mobile applications that track sleep patterns and provide feedback to users to improve sleep quality.</p></p>
