<p><p>UV tape for wafer dicing is a crucial component in the semiconductor industry. It is used to temporarily fix semiconductor wafers during the dicing process. There are two types of UV tape market: single-sided and double-sided. Single-sided UV tape is coated with UV-curable adhesive on one side and is suitable for applications that require minimal residue. On the other hand, double-sided UV tape has adhesive on both sides, providing enhanced adherence and stability for more demanding dicing processes. These variations enable manufacturers to choose the appropriate UV tape based on their specific requirements.</p></p>
