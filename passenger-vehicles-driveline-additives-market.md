<p><p>Passenger Vehicles Driveline Additives market consists of different types of additives that enhance the performance and durability of the driveline system. Dispersants help in maintaining the cleanliness of the system by preventing the formation of sludge and deposits. Viscosity Index Improvers maintain the desired viscosity of the lubricant at various temperatures. Detergents remove dirt and contaminants from the driveline components. Anti-wear agents reduce friction and wear between the moving parts. Antioxidants prevent oxidation and degradation of the lubricant. Corrosion inhibitors protect against rust and corrosion. Friction modifiers minimize friction and improve fuel efficiency. Other additives include pour point depressants, foam inhibitors, and extreme pressure agents.</p></p>
