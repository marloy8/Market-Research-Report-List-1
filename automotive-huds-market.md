<p><strong>The Automotive HUDs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Windshield Projected HUD</li><li>Combiner Projected HUD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive HUDs market consists of two main types: windshield projected HUDs and combiner projected HUDs. Windshield projected HUDs use a small projector to display information, such as speed and navigation directions, directly onto the windshield in the driver's line of sight. Combiner projected HUDs, on the other hand, use a transparent screen or combiner to reflect information from a projector onto a glass panel, typically located above the dashboard. Both types provide drivers with important information without requiring them to shift their focus away from the road.</p></p>
