<p><ul><li>Modified bitumen membrane</li><li>Synthetic polymer waterproofing membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial waterproofing membranes are essential in preventing water from entering buildings. Two types in this market are modified bitumen membranes and synthetic polymer waterproofing membranes. Modified bitumen membranes are made of asphalt mixed with plastic or rubber modifiers, providing elasticity, durability, and resistance to UV rays. Synthetic polymer waterproofing membranes are produced using materials like PVC, TPO, or EPDM, offering excellent durability, flexibility, and resistance to chemicals. Both types ensure protection against water infiltration and are widely used in commercial construction projects to enhance building longevity and structural integrity.</p></p>
