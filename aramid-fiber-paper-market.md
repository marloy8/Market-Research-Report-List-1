<p><p>Aramid fiber paper is classified into two types, para-aramid paper and meta-aramid paper. Para-aramid paper is made from a synthetic fiber known for its high strength and heat resistance properties. It is commonly used in a variety of applications, including electrical insulation. On the other hand, meta-aramid paper is also known for its excellent heat resistance, but it possesses additional flame-resistant qualities, making it suitable for applications such as flame barriers and protective clothing. Both types of aramid fiber paper find extensive use in industries requiring durable and fire-resistant materials.</p></p>
