<p><p>Electric transmission oil pumps can be classified into two types: integrated pump and separate pump. An integrated pump refers to a system where the pump is directly attached to the transmission unit, eliminating the need for additional components. This type of pump offers compactness, improved efficiency, and reduction in weight. On the other hand, a separate pump is a standalone unit that operates independently from the transmission system. This design allows for flexibility in installation, maintenance, and replacement, but may require additional components for optimal performance.</p></p>
