<p><p>The electronic toilet flushes market consists of two main types: fully automatic control and water-saving hybrid control. Fully automatic control toilets have sensors that detect when a user is finished and automatically flush the toilet. These types of toilets provide convenience and hygiene. On the other hand, water-saving hybrid control toilets combine automatic and manual flushing options, giving users the flexibility to choose the water volume for each flush. This helps conserve water while still maintaining user control. Both types of electronic toilet flushes aim to improve efficiency and user experience.</p></p>
