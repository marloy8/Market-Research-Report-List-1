<p><p>Fiber stripping pliers are essential tools in the telecommunications and fiber optic industry. They have different market types, namely single hole and three holes. Single hole pliers have a single blade designed to strip the outer coating of fiber cables, while three holes pliers have three blades for stripping the outer coating, the buffer tube, and the bare fiber. The single hole pliers are suitable for simpler stripping tasks, while the three holes pliers offer versatility and convenience for more complex stripping needs in fiber optic installations.</p></p>
