<p><p>Automotive aftermarket telematics refers to the installation of devices in vehicles to collect and transmit data for various purposes. Plug and Play Telematics involves simple installation by plugging the device into the vehicle's OBD-II port, making it easier to switch between cars. Hardwired Install Telematics requires a more complex installation process involving integrating the device into the vehicle's electrical system. While Plug and Play is more convenient and cost-effective, Hardwired Install offers better security and durability. Both types enable vehicle tracking, diagnostics, and connectivity services.</p></p>
