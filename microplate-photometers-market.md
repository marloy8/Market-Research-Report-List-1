<p><p>Microplate photometers are used in laboratories to measure the absorbance or fluorescence of samples in microplates. There are two main types of microplate photometers: optical filter microplate readers and optical grating microplate readers. Optical filter microplate readers use filters to select specific wavelengths of light for measurement, while optical grating microplate readers use diffraction gratings to disperse light and measure multiple wavelengths simultaneously. Both types of photometers provide valuable data for a wide range of applications in research, clinical diagnostics, and drug discovery.</p></p>
