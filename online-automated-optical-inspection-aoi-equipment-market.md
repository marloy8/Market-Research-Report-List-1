<p><p>The Online Automated Optical Inspection (AOI) Equipment market primarily consists of two types of equipment: 2D AOI Equipment and 3D AOI Equipment. 2D AOI Equipment uses two-dimensional imaging technology to inspect printed circuit boards (PCBs) for defects such as shorts, opens, and misalignments. It captures images of the PCB and compares them to the desired specifications. On the other hand, 3D AOI Equipment utilizes advanced three-dimensional imaging technology to detect more complex defects, including component defects and solder joint quality. Both types of equipment are integral in ensuring the quality and reliability of PCB production processes.</p></p>
