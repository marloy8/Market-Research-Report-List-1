<p><strong>The Polyethylene Rubber Sheets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyethylene rubber sheets are classified into two types: Type I and Type II. Type I sheets are made from low-density polyethylene rubber and have a smoother surface, offering good impact resistance and flexibility. They are commonly used for applications like gaskets, seals, and packaging materials. On the other hand, Type II sheets are made from high-density polyethylene rubber and have a textured surface. They provide better chemical resistance and are often used in industries where resistance to harsh chemicals and solvents is required, such as chemical processing and oil and gas industries.</p></p>
