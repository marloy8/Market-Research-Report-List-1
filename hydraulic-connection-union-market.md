<p><ul><li>Welded Type</li><li>Card Sleeve Type</li><li>Withhold Type</li><li>Flaring Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hydraulic connection unions are available in various market types. The welded type unions are permanently joined by welding, ensuring a strong connection. Card sleeve type unions have a sleeve that slides over the pipes and is tightened with screws, providing a secure fit. Withhold type unions use a restraining ring to hold the pipes together, allowing for easy disconnection. Flaring type unions have flared ends that are connected using a nut and ferrule, ensuring a leak-free joint. Each market type offers different benefits, catering to various needs in the hydraulic industry.</p></p>
