<p><p>The anti-tank guided missiles market can be divided into three types: shoulder-fired, tripod-fired, and vehicle-fired. Shoulder-fired missiles are designed to be carried and launched by a single soldier, providing mobility and flexibility on the battlefield. Tripod-fired missiles, as the name suggests, are launched from a stationary tripod mount, offering stability and accuracy. Vehicle-fired missiles are launched from vehicles such as tanks or armored vehicles, allowing for greater range and firepower. These three types cater to different operational requirements and play a vital role in modern warfare.</p></p>
