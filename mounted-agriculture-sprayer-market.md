<p><ul><li>Engine Drive Sprayer</li><li>Motor Drive Sprayer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mounted agriculture sprayers can be categorized into two main types: engine drive sprayers and motor drive sprayers. Engine drive sprayers are powered by an engine, which can be either gasoline or diesel, providing the necessary energy to operate the sprayer. On the other hand, motor drive sprayers rely on an electric motor for power. The key difference lies in the source of power; engine drive sprayers use internal combustion engines, while motor drive sprayers utilize electric motors for their operation. Overall, both types serve the purpose of spraying agricultural inputs efficiently onto crops.</p></p>
