<p><p>The Vehicle Side Airbag market consists of two main types: Side Torso Airbags and Side Tubular or Curtain Airbags. Side Torso Airbags are located in the sides of the seats, providing protection for the occupants' upper bodies in the event of a collision. On the other hand, Side Tubular or Curtain Airbags are positioned along the roofline or inside the pillars, shielding both the head and torso areas. These airbags are essential safety features that aim to minimize injuries and enhance passenger protection in the event of a side impact crash.</p></p>
