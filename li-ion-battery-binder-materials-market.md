<p><p>Li-ion battery binder materials are crucial components of lithium-ion batteries, enhancing their performance and stability. Anode binder is used to bind active materials onto the anode current collectors, preventing detachment during battery cycling. It ensures efficient electron transfer and enhances conductivity. Cathode binder, on the other hand, securely holds the cathode active materials, improving their adhesion to the current collectors and maintaining structural integrity. Both types of binders are essential for the overall functionality and longevity of Li-ion batteries.</p></p>
