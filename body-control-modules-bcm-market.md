<p><p>Body Control Modules (BCMs) are electronic control units that manage various electrical systems in vehicles. There are two types of BCMs in the market: CAN (Controller Area Network) Body Control Modules and LIN (Local Interconnect Network) Body Control Modules. CAN-based BCMs enable communication between different systems in the vehicle, while LIN-based BCMs are designed for simpler applications and have a lower cost. Both types of BCMs play a crucial role in organizing and coordinating the functioning of various electrical systems such as lighting, seating, door locks, and windows in modern vehicles.</p></p>
