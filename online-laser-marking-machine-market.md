<p><p>The online laser marking machine market is categorized into three types: online fiber laser marking machine, online flying laser marking machine, and online CO2 laser marking machine. Online fiber laser marking machines use fiber as a laser source and are suitable for marking on various materials, including metals and plastics. Online flying laser marking machines are designed to mark moving objects on production lines with high speed and accuracy. Online CO2 laser marking machines use a CO2 laser beam to mark non-metal materials like wood, fabric, and paper.</p></p>
