<p><p>The flue exhaust muffler market consists of two main types: hot flue exhaust mufflers and cold flue exhaust mufflers. Hot flue exhaust mufflers are designed to handle high-temperature flue gases produced by industrial processes, such as those from furnaces or boilers. They are constructed using materials that can withstand extreme heat and are equipped with insulation to minimize heat loss. On the other hand, cold flue exhaust mufflers are used for lower-temperature applications, such as ventilation systems or automotive exhausts, and do not require the same level of heat resistance.</p></p>
