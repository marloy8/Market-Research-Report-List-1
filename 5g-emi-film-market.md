<p><p>The market for 5G EMI (Electromagnetic Interference) film can be broadly categorized into two types: metal-mesh film and coating film. Metal-mesh film is a transparent film with a fine metal mesh pattern embedded within it. It offers excellent EMI shielding capabilities and is widely used in electronic devices. On the other hand, coating film is a protective layer applied on electronic components to enhance their EMI resistance. It acts as a barrier against electromagnetic waves and is used in various industries, such as automotive and telecommunications.</p></p>
