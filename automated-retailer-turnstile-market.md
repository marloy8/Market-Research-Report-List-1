<p><p>Automated retailer turnstiles are categorized into various types based on their design and functionality. Arm turnstiles are the most common type, featuring rotating arms that allow one person access at a time. Swing gates operate similarly, but have a full barrier swing instead of arms. Waist-high helicopter turnstiles have rotating top arms that form a barrier at waist height. Other types include tripod turnstiles, optical turnstiles, and drop arm turnstiles. Each type offers different levels of security and access control for automated retail environments.</p></p>
