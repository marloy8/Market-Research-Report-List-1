<p><p>Lithography overcoats market encompasses two main types - topcoats and overcoats. Topcoats refer to a protective layer applied on the top of the lithographic materials to enhance the durability and surface properties of the substrate. They provide resistance against moisture, chemicals, and physical abrasion. On the other hand, overcoats are utilized as an additional layer to improve the performance of the lithographic process. They can modify the surface energy of the substrate, ensuring better ink adhesion and reducing defects like sticking or tearing during printing.</p></p>
