<p><p>Ball mill liners are protective linings installed in the inner surface of the cylindrical shell of a ball mill to enhance its performance and lifespan. Rubber mill liners are made of rubber, which is resistant to wear, making them ideal for applications where abrasion is a concern. Metal mill liners, on the other hand, are usually made of alloyed steel and are used in heavy-duty applications where high impact and grinding forces are present. Both types of liners provide protection to the mill shell, reduce power consumption, and improve grinding efficiency.</p></p>
