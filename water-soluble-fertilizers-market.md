<p><strong>The Water-Soluble Fertilizers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Nitrogenous</li><li>Phosphatic</li><li>Potassic</li><li>Micronutrients</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water-soluble fertilizers are categorized into four main types. Nitrogenous fertilizers are rich in nitrogen, a crucial nutrient for plant growth and development. Phosphatic fertilizers contain high levels of phosphorous, which supports root growth, flowering, and fruiting. Potassic fertilizers are potassium-based and aid in overall plant health, disease resistance, and nutrient absorption. Micronutrients, including iron, copper, zinc, and manganese, are necessary in small quantities for essential plant functions. All these fertilizer types can dissolve in water, making them easily absorbed by plants for optimal growth and yield.</p></p>
