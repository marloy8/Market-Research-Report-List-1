<p><ul><li>Gen. 1 Bearing</li><li>Gen. 2 Bearing</li><li>Gen. 3 Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive hub bearings, also known as automotive wheel bearings, have evolved over time into three generations. Gen. 1 bearings are simple, integrated units consisting of an outer ring, inner ring, steel balls, and a cage. Gen. 2 bearings eliminate the need for the inner ring by incorporating it into the hub assembly. They often include wheel speed sensors. Gen. 3 bearings integrate additional functions such as anti-lock braking system (ABS) and traction control system (TCS) sensors. Each generation represents improvements in design and functionality, enhancing vehicle performance and safety.</p></p>
