<p><p>Solid Bleached Board (SBB) is a type of paperboard that is used for packaging and printing applications. It comes in two market types - 1-Sided and 2-Sided. In the 1-Sided market, only one side of the board is coated, leaving the other side uncoated. This makes it suitable for applications where only one side needs to be printed on or have a glossy finish.
