<p><ul><li>Semi-graphitic Type</li><li>Graphitic Type</li><li>Graphitized Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cathode blocks are a crucial component in the aluminum production process. There are three main types of cathode blocks: semi-graphitic type, graphitic type, and graphitized type. The semi-graphitic type is made from a mixture of petroleum coke and coal pitch, offering moderate electrical and thermal conductivity. Graphitic type cathode blocks are composed of high-quality graphite and exhibit excellent conductivity properties. On the other hand, graphitized type cathode blocks are processed at high temperatures, enhancing their mechanical strength and electrical conductivity, making them ideal for long-lasting usage in the aluminum industry.</p></p>
