<p><p>Fan-Out packaging refers to a type of packaging technology used to increase the performance and functionality of integrated circuits. There are two main types: Core Fan-Out Packaging and High-Density Fan-Out Packaging. Core Fan-Out Packaging involves redistributing the active circuitry components into a larger area, allowing for increased functionality and performance. On the other hand, High-Density Fan-Out Packaging involves placing the circuitry components closer together, resulting in a higher density of components, thereby increasing processing power and reducing overall size. Both types aim to enhance the capabilities of integrated circuits for various electronic applications.</p></p>
