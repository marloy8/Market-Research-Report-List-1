<p><p>The Mobile Access Control Platform market involves two notable types: NFC and Bluetooth. NFC, or Near Field Communication, enables devices to communicate when placed nearby. In the context of access control, NFC allows users to gain entry by simply tapping their mobile device on a compatible reader. On the other hand, Bluetooth market refers to the utilization of Bluetooth technology for access control purposes. This enables users to connect their smartphones to access control systems wirelessly, granting entry or performing other related tasks using their mobile devices.</p></p>
