<p><p>Inert Gas Filled Thermometers have two main types in the market: Rigid Stem Type and Capillary Type. </p><p>Rigid stem type thermometers consist of a straight stem that contains the inert gas. They are commonly used for measuring temperatures in industrial applications where the thermometer needs to be inserted into a solid or fixed position.</p><p>Capillary type thermometers have a coiled capillary tube filled with inert gas. These thermometers are used in applications where the temperature needs to be measured remotely or in hard-to-reach places, as the capillary tube can be extended to the desired location.</p></p>
