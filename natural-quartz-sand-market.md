<p><p>Natural quartz sand is available in different market types, including dry process production and water production. This type of production method is more cost-effective and widely used in various industries. On the other hand, water production involves the extraction of quartz sand from rivers and lakes using dredging or pumping methods. This market type is preferred when the sand requires minimum impurities and requires further processing to meet specific quality requirements.</p></p>
