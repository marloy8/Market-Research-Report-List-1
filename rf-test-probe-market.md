<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>RF Test Probes are electronic devices used to measure and test radio frequency signals. Passive Type probes do not require external power and are ideal for low-frequency applications. They are cost-effective but have limited frequency response and signal attenuation. Active Type probes, on the other hand, require external power and have a built-in amplifier, allowing them to detect and amplify weak signals. These probes have a wider frequency range and higher sensitivity, making them suitable for high-frequency applications but at a higher cost.</p></p>
