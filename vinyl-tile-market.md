Vinyl tile offers a wide range of design options and is available in various colors, patterns, and textures, making it an attractive choice for both residential and commercial applications. Additionally, vinyl tile is a sustainable and environmentally friendly flooring option as it can be recycled and reused.</p><p>The market analysis for vinyl tile indicates a positive growth trajectory. The rising population and urbanization, along with the increasing construction activities worldwide, are key drivers of market expansion.
