<p><p>The fleet tracking and monitoring software market is divided into two main types: professional software and managed software. Professional software refers to the software that is purchased and operated by the fleet owners themselves. It provides them with the tools and features to track and monitor their own fleet of vehicles. On the other hand, managed software is a service provided by third-party vendors or fleet management companies. They take care of the software setup, maintenance, and data analysis on behalf of the fleet owners, offering a more hands-off approach to fleet tracking and monitoring.</p></p>
