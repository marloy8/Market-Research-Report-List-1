<p><strong>The Rear View Camera Lens Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CCD Cameras</li><li>CMOS Cameras</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rear view camera lens market comprises of CCD cameras and CMOS cameras. CCD cameras use charge-coupled device technology to convert light into an electric charge, which is then processed to form an image. They offer better image quality and sensitivity in low light conditions. On the other hand, CMOS cameras use complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor technology, which provides better power efficiency and faster data transfer. They are generally more affordable compared to CCD cameras but may have lower image quality and sensitivity. Both types serve the purpose of providing a clear rear view for vehicles.</p></p>
