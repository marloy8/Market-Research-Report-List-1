<p><p>Silicone fluids are widely used in various industries for their unique properties. The market for silicone fluids can be categorized into two types: modified silicone fluids and straight silicone fluids. Modified silicone fluids are those that have been chemically modified to enhance specific properties such as viscosity, thermal stability, or compatibility with other substances. On the other hand, straight silicone fluids are unmodified and are known for their excellent dielectric properties, high thermal stability, and resistance to oxidation. Both types of silicone fluids have diverse applications across industries like automotive, personal care, and electronics.</p></p>
