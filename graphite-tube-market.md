<p><strong>The Graphite Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resin Bonded</li><li>Carbon Bonded Graphite</li><li>Fully Graphitized</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Graphite tube market types refer to different forms of graphite used in various industries. Resin bonded graphite tubes are made by mixing graphite with resin and have good thermal conductivity, but lower mechanical strength. Carbon bonded graphite tubes are produced by mixing graphite with carbonaceous materials, resulting in higher mechanical strength but lower thermal conductivity. Fully graphitized graphite tubes undergo a high-temperature process that converts all carbon to graphite, providing excellent mechanical and thermal properties. These different types cater to various industrial applications and offer varying levels of performance in terms of conductivity and strength.</p></p>
