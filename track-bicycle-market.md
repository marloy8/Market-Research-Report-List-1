<p><p>A track bicycle, also known as a track bike or velodrome bicycle, is a type of bicycle that is specifically designed for track cycling, a form of bicycle racing held on a velodrome track. They are built for speed and agility, allowing cyclists to achieve high speeds and maneuverability on the track.</p><p>The current outlook for the track bicycle market is positive, with a growing interest in track cycling as a sport and recreational activity.
