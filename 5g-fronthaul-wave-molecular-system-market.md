<p><p>The 5G Fronthaul Wave Molecular System market comprises three types: Coarse Wavelength Division Multiplexing (CWDM), Long Wavelength Division Multiplexing (LWDM), and Medium Wavelength Division Multiplexing (MWDM). CWDM uses wider channels to transmit multiple signals simultaneously, enabling cost-effective and efficient data transfer. LWDM operates over longer distances and offers more bandwidth capacity through the use of different wavelengths. MWDM provides higher capacity by dividing the optical spectrum into smaller channels. These different types facilitate diverse transmission requirements in the 5G fronthaul network, catering to various demands and optimizing communication efficiency.</p></p>
