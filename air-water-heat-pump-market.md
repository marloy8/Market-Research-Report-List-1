<p><p>The Air-Water Heat Pump market can be classified into three main types: Low Temperature Type, High Temperature Type, and Hybrid Type. The Low Temperature Type heat pump is suitable for heating water at lower temperatures, typically used for underfloor heating or space heating. The High Temperature Type is designed to provide hot water at higher temperatures, suitable for domestic hot water applications. Lastly, the Hybrid Type combines both air-water and water-water heat pumps, offering versatility and energy efficiency by utilizing multiple heat sources.</p></p>
