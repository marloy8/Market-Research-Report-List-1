<p><p>High performance polymers market is categorized into various types including fluoro polymers, liquid crystal polymers, polyamides, polyimides, and polyketones. Fluoro polymers offer resistance to chemicals, weathering, and high temperatures. Liquid crystal polymers have excellent mechanical, thermal, and electrical properties, making them suitable for applications like electrical connectors. Polyamides have exceptional strength and can withstand high temperatures, making them ideal for automotive and electrical industries. Polyimides possess excellent thermal stability and are used in aerospace and electronic applications. Polyketones provide high chemical resistance and are extensively utilized in automotive and oil and gas industries.</p></p>
