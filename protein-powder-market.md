<p><p>Protein powder is popular among fitness enthusiasts and athletes for its ability to support muscle recovery and growth. There are several types of protein powders available in the market. </p><p>Whey protein is the most common and is derived from milk. It is known for its fast absorption rate and high amino acid content. </p><p>Casein protein, also derived from milk, is digested more slowly, providing a steady release of amino acids into the bloodstream.</p><p>Soy protein is plant-based and suitable for vegetarians and vegans. It contains all nine essential amino acids.</p><p>Hemp protein, derived from hemp seeds, is also plant-based and rich in omega-3 fatty acids.</p><p>Other types may include pea protein, rice protein, and blended proteins which combine multiple sources for a balanced amino acid profile.</p></p>
