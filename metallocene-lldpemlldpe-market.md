<p><p>Metallocene LLDPE (mLLDPE) is a type of linear low-density polyethylene produced using metallocene catalysts. It is increasingly being used in various applications due to its superior properties such as excellent optical clarity, high tensile strength, and good puncture resistance. There are three major market types based on the type of comonomer used in the production of mLLDPE: C4-mLLDPE, C6-mLLDPE, and C8-mLLDPE. C4-mLLDPE is made with a butene comonomer, C6-mLLDPE with a hexene comonomer, and C8-mLLDPE with an octene comonomer. The choice of comonomer affects the film properties and end-use applications of mLLDPE.</p></p>
