<p><p>The Automotive Blowers market includes two major types: Single Wheel Blower and Double Wheel Blower. Single Wheel Blower refers to a blower system containing only one wheel for air circulation. It is commonly used in smaller vehicles or applications where space is limited. On the other hand, Double Wheel Blower consists of two wheels operating simultaneously to enhance air circulation. It is typically employed in larger vehicles or applications requiring greater airflow. Both types play a crucial role in automotive systems by providing efficient air movement for ventilation, cooling, and heating purposes.</p></p>
