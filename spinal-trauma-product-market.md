<p><ul><li>Spinal Fusion</li><li>Non-fusion Products</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The spinal trauma product market consists of two main types: spinal fusion products and non-fusion products. Spinal fusion involves the joining of two or more vertebrae, using implants such as rods, screws, or interbody cages, to stabilize the spine and promote fusion. Non-fusion products, on the other hand, do not involve joining vertebrae but focus on providing dynamic stabilization or artificial disc replacement to maintain normal spinal motion. Both types of products aim to treat spinal trauma and restore stability and function to the spine.</p></p>
