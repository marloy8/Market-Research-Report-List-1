<p><ul><li>Single Energy Mode</li><li>Dual Energy Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the air-powered vehicle market, there are two types of market modes: Single Energy Mode and Dual Energy Mode. In Single Energy Mode, vehicles solely use air as the source of energy to operate. These vehicles typically have compressed air tanks that power the engine and provide propulsion. On the other hand, Dual Energy Mode vehicles use a combination of air and another source of energy, such as gasoline or electricity. This allows for increased power and range, as the secondary energy source provides additional energy for driving the vehicle.</p></p>
