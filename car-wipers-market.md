<p><p>The car wiper market consists of two main types: ordinary wipers and boneless wipers. Ordinary wipers are traditional wiper blades with a metal frame that holds the rubber blade in place. They are cost-effective and widely used in the market. On the other hand, boneless wipers are advanced wiper blades without any metal frame, providing a sleek and aerodynamic design. They are more expensive but offer improved wiping performance and durability. Both types serve the purpose of clearing rain or debris from a vehicle's windshield to ensure better visibility while driving.</p></p>
