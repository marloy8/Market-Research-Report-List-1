<p><strong>The Chemical Mechanical Polishing Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chemical Mechanical Polishing (CMP) Materials Market is segmented into Type I and Type II. Type I refers to the spherical inorganic particles used as an abrasive material in CMP, such as fumed silica and ceria abrasives. These particles have a narrow size distribution and are mainly used for advanced applications, providing high planarization efficiency. On the other hand, Type II includes non-spherical inorganic particles like colloidal silica and alumina abrasives. These particles have a wider size distribution and are primarily utilized for less demanding applications in CMP, offering moderate planarization capability.</p></p>
