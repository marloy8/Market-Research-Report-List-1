<p><p>An electric brake booster is a device utilized in vehicles to enhance the brake system's performance by applying pressure to the brake pedal. Electric brake boosters are considered an advanced replacement for traditional brake booster systems as they provide quicker and more precise braking response.</p><p>The electric brake booster market is expected to grow at a significant rate during the forecast period. <p><p>The Electric Brake Booster market can be categorized into two types: Two-Box and One-Box. In the Two-Box market, the electric braking system consists of separate components, such as the booster and the ECU (Electronic Control Unit). These components are installed separately, offering flexibility and easier maintenance. On the other hand, the One-Box market integrates the booster and the ECU into a single unit. This compact design reduces space requirements and simplifies installation, making it suitable for vehicles with limited space.</p></p>
