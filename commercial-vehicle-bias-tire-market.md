<p><p>Commercial vehicle bias tires are a type of tire designed specifically for commercial vehicles. There are two main types: ordinary bias tires and bias belted tires. Ordinary bias tires are constructed with multiple layers of nylon cords laid diagonally in opposite directions. They have a strong casing which offers good puncture resistance and durability, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. On the other hand, bias belted tires have an additional layer of belts made of steel or fiberglass added under the tread. This improves stability, handling, and load-carrying capacity, making them ideal for long-haul and high-speed operations.</p></p>
