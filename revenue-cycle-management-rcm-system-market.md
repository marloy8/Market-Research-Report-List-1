<p><p>Revenue Cycle Management (RCM) systems are software solutions used in the healthcare industry to manage the financial aspects of patient billing and collections. There are three main types of RCM systems: web-based, on-premise, and cloud-based. </p><p>Web-based RCM systems are accessed through internet browsers, allowing users to access and manage financial data from anywhere with an internet connection. On-premise RCM systems are installed locally on a healthcare organization's servers, providing greater control and customization but requiring maintenance and IT infrastructure. Cloud-based RCM systems store and process data on remote servers, offering scalability, automatic updates, and data security without the need for physical servers.</p></p>
