<p><p>EP additives stand for Extreme Pressure additives, which are used in lubricants to reduce friction and prevent wear and tear in metal surfaces under high-pressure conditions. These additives form a protective film on metal surfaces, enhancing their load-carrying capacity and durability. On the other hand, AW additives, also known as Anti-Wear additives, are used to minimize wear and tear in lubricated parts by creating a protective film and reducing friction. Both EP and AW additives play a crucial role in improving the performance and longevity of lubricants in various industrial applications.</p></p>
