<p><p>The Electric Remote Control Panels Market comprises two main types: Low Tension Control Panels and High Tension Control Panels. </p><p>Low Tension Control Panels are used for controlling and regulating electrical equipment that operates at a voltage below 1kV. These panels are commonly found in residential, commercial, and small-scale industrial applications. </p><p>On the other hand, High Tension Control Panels are designed to handle voltage levels above 1kV and are mainly used in large-scale industrial applications such as power plants and heavy machinery. These panels provide the necessary control and protection for equipment operating at high voltage levels.</p></p>
