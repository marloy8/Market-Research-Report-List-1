<p><p>The drilling mud market is categorized into different types, including Oil-Based Fluids (OBF), Water-Based Fluids (WBF), and Others. Oil-based fluids are drilling muds where oil is used as the base fluid and are preferred for their thermal stability and lubrication properties. Water-based fluids use water as the base fluid and are environmentally friendly, but can be less effective in certain drilling operations. The "Others" category includes various types of drilling muds, such as synthetic-based fluids or foam-based fluids, which offer specific advantages for different drilling conditions.</p></p>
