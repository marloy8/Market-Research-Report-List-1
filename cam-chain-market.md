<p><p>The cam chain market is divided into various types such as roller chain and silent chain. Roller chain is a type of cam chain that is commonly used in high-speed applications. It consists of cylindrical rollers connected by side links, ensuring smooth and efficient power transmission. On the other hand, silent chain is another type of cam chain that operates quietly due to its unique design, which involves alternating links with protruding pins and through-links. Both roller chain and silent chain serve the purpose of transferring rotational force effectively in different applications.</p></p>
