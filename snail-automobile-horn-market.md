<p><p>The Snail Automobile Horn Market is primarily divided into two types: electronic horns and electromagnetic horns. Electronic horns operate using electronic circuitry and produce sound through a speaker or diaphragm. They are compact, lightweight, and offer various sound options. On the other hand, electromagnetic horns create sound through an electromagnet that vibrates a metal diaphragm. They are known for their loud and powerful output but tend to be bulkier and heavier. Both types cater to the automotive industry, providing effective and audible warning signals for drivers and pedestrians.</p></p>
