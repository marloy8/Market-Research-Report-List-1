<p><p>Thin layer chromatography (TLC) systems are used for separating and analyzing different chemicals in a sample. Classical TLC is the traditional method, which uses a stationary phase and a mobile phase to separate the components. High-performance TLC (HPTLC) is an advanced version that provides better resolution and sensitivity. Preparative TLC (PLC) is used for large-scale separation and purification of compounds. These different types of TLC systems cater to various applications and requirements, offering different levels of performance and functionality for accurate analysis and purification of chemicals.</p></p>
