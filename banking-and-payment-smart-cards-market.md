<p><p>The banking and payment smart cards market consists of two main types: contact-based smart cards and contactless smart cards. </p><p>Contact-based smart cards require physical contact between the card and the card reading device for information transfer. They have a small chip embedded in them, which holds the cardholder's data and enables secure transactions.</p><p>On the other hand, contactless smart cards use radio frequency identification (RFID) technology to enable transactions without physical contact. These cards can be waved or tapped near the card reader to complete the transaction swiftly and conveniently.</p></p>

<p><strong>What are the Emerging Trends in the Global Banking and Payment Smart Cards market?</strong></p>
<p><p>The global banking and payment smart cards market is experiencing several emerging trends that are reshaping the industry.
