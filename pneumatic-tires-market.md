On the other hand, air pneumatics are filled with compressed air, allowing them to provide a comfortable and cushioned ride. These tires are often used in passenger vehicles, bicycles, and other light-duty applications. Both types offer distinct advantages depending on the intended use and help enhance the overall performance and safety of vehicles.</p></p>
