SPS involves the synthesis of peptides in a solution, which allows for rapid and efficient production. On the other hand, SPPS involves the stepwise assembly of peptides on a solid support, which ensures higher purity and quality control. Both methods are widely used in the peptide vaccine market, offering different advantages depending on the specific requirements of the vaccine development process.</p></p>
