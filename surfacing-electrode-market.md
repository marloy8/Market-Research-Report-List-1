<p><p>The surfacing electrode market encompasses two main types: hardfacing electrode and conventional hardfacing electrode. Hardfacing electrodes are specially designed to provide a protective layer on metal surfaces that are subject to wear, abrasion, and impact. They are used primarily in industries such as mining, construction, and agriculture. Meanwhile, conventional hardfacing electrodes are more commonly used for overlaying and rebuilding worn-out parts. These electrodes offer good hardness and resistance to corrosion, making them suitable for various applications, including machinery repair, manufacturing, and maintenance of industrial equipment.</p></p>
