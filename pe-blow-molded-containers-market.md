<p><p>PE Blow Molded Containers are made from either High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) or Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE). HDPE containers are known for their strength, durability and resistance to chemicals, making them ideal for storing and transporting hazardous materials. LDPE containers, on the other hand, are more flexible and have a higher impact resistance, making them suitable for products that need to be squeezed out or dispensed. Both HDPE and LDPE containers are widely used in industries such as pharmaceuticals, food and beverages, chemicals, and cosmetics.</p></p>
