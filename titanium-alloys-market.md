<p><p>Titanium alloys are widely used in various industries due to their superior strength, low weight, and corrosion resistance. The Ti 6AL-4V alloy is the most common titanium alloy, composed of 6% aluminum and 4% vanadium. Ti 6AL ELI is a variation of Ti 6AL-4V with extra low interstitial elements, making it suitable for medical applications. Ti 3Al 2.5 is a near-alpha alloy with excellent weldability and high strength-to-weight ratio. Ti 5Al-2.5Sn contains 5% aluminum and 2.5% tin, providing improved weldability and resistance to stress corrosion cracking.</p></p>
