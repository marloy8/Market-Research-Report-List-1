The market is also driven by the growing awareness among consumers regarding the benefits of sisal fibers, as they are biodegradable, renewable, and have low environmental impact.</p><p>In recent years, there has been a rise in the adoption of sisal fiber in the automotive industry for the production of lightweight components, as it helps in reducing fuel consumption and lowering CO2 emissions.
