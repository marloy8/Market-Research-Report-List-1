<p><p>The glass fiber filters and prefilters market is categorized into three types: ASHRAE, HEPA, and ULPA. ASHRAE filters are designed to fulfil the requirements set by the American Society of Heating, Refrigerating and Air-Conditioning Engineers. HEPA (High-Efficiency Particulate Air) filters provide a higher level of air filtration, capturing smaller particles. ULPA (Ultra-Low Penetration Air) filters offer the highest level of air filtration, removing even smaller particles than HEPA filters. These filters are widely used in industries where air quality and removal of contaminants are of utmost importance.</p></p>
