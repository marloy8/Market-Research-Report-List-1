Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) maintains a safe distance from other vehicles by automatically adjusting the motorcycle's speed. Anti-Lock Braking System (ABS) prevents wheel lock-up during braking, reducing the risk of accidents. Other technologies in this market may include lane departure warning systems, blind-spot detection, and electronic stability control, all aimed at improving overall motorcycle safety.</p></p>
