<p><p>Flat glass is a versatile material used in multiple industries. Ordinary flat glass is produced by cooling molten glass on a flat metal surface, resulting in a smooth and polishable surface. Float glass is a type of ordinary flat glass created by floating molten glass onto a bed of molten metal, resulting in a pristine surface without distortion. Rolled glass, on the other hand, is produced by passing molten glass between two rollers, creating a textured surface suitable for privacy or decorative purposes.</p></p>
