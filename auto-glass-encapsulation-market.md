<p><p>Auto glass encapsulation is a process that involves sealing the edges of automotive glass to provide support, reduce noise, and prevent water leakage. There are different types of materials used for this purpose: TPE (Thermoplastic Elastomer) offers flexibility and resilience, PUR (Polyurethane) provides excellent adhesion and durability, EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) offers resistance to weathering and UV radiation, and PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) provides good sealing properties. Each material has its unique characteristics and benefits, catering to different automotive requirements and ensuring a reliable and efficient encapsulation solution.</p></p>
