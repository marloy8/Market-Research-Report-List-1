<p><p>The iron casting market is divided into three main types: gray iron, ductile iron, and malleable iron. Gray iron is the most commonly used type due to its low cost and good thermal conductivity. Ductile iron is known for its high strength and ductility, making it suitable for applications requiring toughness. Malleable iron is a form of cast iron that has been heat-treated to increase its ductility and toughness. These types cater to different market needs, offering various properties and benefits for specific applications.</p></p>
