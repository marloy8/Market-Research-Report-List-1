<p><ul><li>Digital Laser Sensor</li><li>CMOS Laser Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laser sensor market consists of two primary types: digital laser sensors and CMOS laser sensors. A digital laser sensor utilizes a laser beam to detect the presence or absence of an object, converting the received signal into a digital output. On the other hand, CMOS laser sensors employ a complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor image sensor to capture images and detect objects based on the reflected laser light. Both types are widely used in various industries for tasks such as distance measurement, object detection, and position control.</p></p>
