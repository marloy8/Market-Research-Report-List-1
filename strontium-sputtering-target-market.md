<p><p>The strontium sputtering target market includes two main types: rotary type and non-rotatable type. Rotary type targets are designed to rotate during the sputtering process, which ensures uniform erosion and prolongs the target's lifespan. These targets are commonly used in large-scale manufacturing processes. On the other hand, non-rotatable type targets are stationary during sputtering and are typically used in smaller-scale or research applications where high precision and stability are essential. Both types of targets play a crucial role in various industries, including electronics, semiconductors, solar cells, and display technologies.</p></p>
