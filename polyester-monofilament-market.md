<p><p>Polyester Monofilament is a type of synthetic fiber with various types available in the market. The three main types are PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate), PBT (Polybutylene Terephthalate), and PEN (Polyethylene Naphthalate). PET monofilament is known for its excellent resistance to stretching and chemicals. PBT monofilament has a good balance of strength and flexibility, making it suitable for various applications. PEN monofilament is highly resistant to heat, chemicals, and mechanical stresses. All three types of polyester monofilament offer unique characteristics, catering to different needs in industries such as textiles, fishing, and filtration.</p></p>
