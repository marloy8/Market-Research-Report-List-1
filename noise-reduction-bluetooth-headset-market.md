<p><p>The noise reduction Bluetooth headset market consists of two types: active noise reduction and passive noise reduction. Active noise reduction headsets incorporate technology that actively cancels out external noise, creating a quieter environment for the user. They utilize microphones to detect ambient noise and generate anti-noise signals to counteract it. On the other hand, passive noise reduction headsets rely on physical barriers and sound insulation materials to block out noise. These headsets are designed to reduce noise passively without the need for electronic components.</p></p>
