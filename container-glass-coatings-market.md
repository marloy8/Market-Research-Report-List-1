<p><p>Container glass coatings market includes two major types: water-based and oil-based coatings. Water-based coatings are formulated with water as a key component and are considered environmentally friendly as they have low VOC emissions. These coatings are popular for their excellent adhesion and resistance to chemicals, making them ideal for packaging sensitive products. On the other hand, oil-based coatings are formulated with oil-based resins, offering enhanced durability and scratch resistance. They are known for their glossy finish, making them suitable for premium packaging applications.</p></p>
