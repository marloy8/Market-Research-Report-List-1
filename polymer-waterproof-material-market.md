<p><p>The market for polymer waterproof materials can be categorized into three main types: waterproofing membranes, waterproofing coatings, and others. Waterproofing membranes are thin sheets made of polymers that are used to create an impermeable barrier, preventing water from penetrating surfaces. Waterproofing coatings, on the other hand, are liquid or semi-liquid polymers that can be applied as a protective layer to surfaces. The "others" category includes various polymer-based waterproofing products such as sealants, adhesives, and sprays that provide waterproofing properties to different surfaces and materials.</p></p>
