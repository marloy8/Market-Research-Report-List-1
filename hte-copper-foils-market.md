<p><p>The copper foils market can be divided into three categories based on thickness. Copper foils below 15 μm are thin and lightweight, making them suitable for applications requiring flexibility and miniaturization, such as consumer electronics and communication devices. Copper foils in the range of 15-30 μm offer a balance between flexibility and strength, commonly used in printed circuit boards for automotive and industrial applications. Copper foils above 30 μm are thicker and provide enhanced conductivity and durability, commonly used in power transmission and storage applications.</p></p>
