<p><p>The Carbon Black Feed Stock Market is classified into four types: Coal Tar, Ethylene Tar, Local Decant, and US Decant. Coal Tar refers to the by-product obtained during coal carbonization, while Ethylene Tar is the residual liquid produced from steam cracking of hydrocarbons. Local Decant is the residue from refining heavy oils and is available in regional markets. US Decant refers to the residual oils produced during the refining process and is primarily available in the United States. These different types of feedstock provide diverse sources for the production of carbon black.</p></p>
