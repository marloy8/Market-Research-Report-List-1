<p><p>The automotive cooling fan belt market encompasses three main types: triangle type, wedge belt type, and timing belt type. The triangle type is a V-belt with a triangular cross-section that provides efficient power transmission. The wedge belt type is a wider version of the triangle type, offering higher horsepower capacity and reduced slippage. Lastly, the timing belt type uses a toothed design, allowing synchronized rotation between the engine's crankshaft and camshaft. Each type is designed to fulfill specific requirements, providing options for various automotive cooling fan systems.</p></p>
