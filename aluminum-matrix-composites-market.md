<p><p>Aluminum Matrix Composites (AMCs) market includes two predominant types: Particle Enhanced Type and Fiber Enhanced Type. Particle Enhanced Type AMCs involve the incorporation of ceramic particles into the aluminum matrix, enhancing properties like hardness and wear resistance. On the other hand, Fiber Enhanced Type AMCs utilize reinforced fibers (usually made of materials like carbon or silicon carbide) that are embedded in the aluminum matrix, enhancing mechanical strength and stiffness. Both types of AMCs have various industrial applications, including automotive, aerospace, and electrical industries.</p></p>
