<p><p>The automotive flasher market consists of two main types: electronic flashers and thermal flashers. Electronic flashers utilize electronic components to control the flashing rate of vehicle indicators, such as turn signals and hazard lights. They are known for their durability, accuracy, and compatibility with modern vehicle electronics. On the other hand, thermal flashers use bi-metallic strips or thermostats to control the flashing rate. They are more common in older vehicles and offer a simpler and cost-effective solution. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining visual communication and safety on the roads.</p></p>
