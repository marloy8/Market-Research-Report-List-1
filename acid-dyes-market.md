<p><strong>The Acid Dyes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Strong Acid Dyes</li><li>Weak Acid Dyes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The acid dyes market can be divided into two main types: strong acid dyes and weak acid dyes. Strong acid dyes are highly soluble in water and have a strong affinity for protein fibers, such as wool, silk, and leather. They provide intense and vibrant colors with excellent light and wash fastness. In contrast, weak acid dyes have lower water solubility and affinity for protein fibers. They are mainly used for dyeing synthetic fibers like nylon and acetate, offering moderate colorfastness. Both types play a significant role in the textile and leather industries.</p></p>
