<p><p>The fuel cell buses market includes various types such as PEMFC (Proton Exchange Membrane Fuel Cell) forklifts, DMFC (Direct Methanol Fuel Cell) forklifts, and others. PEMFC forklifts use a Proton Exchange Membrane to transfer hydrogen ions from the anode to the cathode, generating electricity. DMFC forklifts utilize direct methanol fuel cells, which use a methanol solution and a catalyst to produce electricity. Lastly, the "others" category encompasses alternative types of fuel cell buses, such as solid oxide fuel cell (SOFC) forklifts, alkaline fuel cell (AFC) forklifts, and phosphoric acid fuel cell (PAFC) forklifts.</p></p>
