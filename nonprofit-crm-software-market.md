<p><ul><li>Cloud Based</li><li>Premise Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nonprofit CRM software market can be categorized into two types: cloud-based and premise-based. Cloud-based CRM software is hosted on external servers and accessed through the internet. It offers flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness as it eliminates the need for internal infrastructure. On the other hand, premise-based CRM software is installed directly on the organization's servers, offering greater control and security. It requires internal IT infrastructure and resources for maintenance. The choice between these types depends on the organization's specific requirements, budget, and level of control desired.</p></p>
