<p><strong>The Neoprene Adhesives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Type</li><li>Emulsion Type</li><li>Solvent-Free Liquid Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Neoprene Adhesives Market is categorized into three types: Solvent Type, Emulsion Type, and Solvent-Free Liquid Type. </p><p>Solvent Type neoprene adhesives use solvents like toluene or methyl ethyl ketone to dissolve the neoprene polymer and create a strong adhesive solution. </p><p>Emulsion Type neoprene adhesives use water as a solvent to form a stable emulsion with the neoprene polymer.</p><p>Solvent-Free Liquid Type neoprene adhesives are free from solvents and are usually supplied as ready-to-use liquids. They offer good adhesion properties without the need for harmful solvents.</p></p>
