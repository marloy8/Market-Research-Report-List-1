<p><p>Waterproofing systems refer to materials and techniques used to protect structures from water infiltration. The market consists of three main types: waterproofing membranes, which are thin layers applied on surfaces to prevent water penetration; waterproofing coatings, applied as liquid or spray to create a waterproof barrier; and injection grouting, a process of filling gaps or cracks with waterproofing material to seal against water leakage. These systems play a crucial role in preventing water damage and maintaining the integrity of buildings, infrastructure, and other constructions.</p></p>
