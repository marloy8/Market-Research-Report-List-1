<p><p>High purity polycrystalline silicon (HP polysilicon) is a product of the solar industry and is primarily used as a raw material in the production of solar cells. The development of advanced manufacturing technologies for HP polysilicon production, such as fluidized bed reactors and Siemens process, is improving the efficiency and reducing production costs.
