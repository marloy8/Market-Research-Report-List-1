<p><p>The intelligent vehicle undercarriage scanner market can be divided into two types: fixed and mobile. Fixed scanners are permanently installed at specific locations, such as entrances or checkpoints, and vehicles pass over them for undercarriage scanning. Mobile scanners, on the other hand, are portable and can be moved to different locations as needed. They are typically mounted on vehicles or trailers and can be deployed at different sites for scanning vehicles on the go. Both types serve the purpose of detecting any suspicious objects, explosives, or unauthorized items beneath vehicles for security purposes.</p></p>
